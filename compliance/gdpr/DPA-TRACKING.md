## DPA Template Library

### Template 1: Our Standard DPA (Send to Vendors)

**Location:** `/compliance/gdpr/templates/STANDARD-DPA-TEMPLATE.md` (to be created)

**Key Clauses Required:**
1. **Scope of Processing** (Art. 28(3)(a))
   - Subject matter and duration
   - Nature and purpose of processing
   - Type of personal data
   - Categories of data subjects

2. **Processor Obligations** (Art. 28(3)(a-h))
   - Process only on documented instructions
   - Confidentiality obligations
   - Security measures (Art. 32)
   - Sub-processor authorization and oversight
   - Assistance with data subject rights (Art. 15-22)
   - Deletion or return of data
   - Audit and inspection rights
   - Immediate breach notification (<24 hours)

3. **Data Subject Rights Assistance**
   - Access requests (Art. 15)
   - Rectification (Art. 16)
   - Erasure (Art. 17)
   - Restriction of processing (Art. 18)
   - Data portability (Art. 20)
   - Objection (Art. 21)

4. **Security Requirements** (Art. 32)
   - Encryption at rest and in transit
   - Pseudonymization where applicable
   - Confidentiality, integrity, availability
   - Regular security testing
   - Incident response procedures

5. **Sub-Processors**
   - General authorization for disclosed sub-processors
   - 30-day notice for new sub-processors
   - Objection rights
   - Flow-down of obligations

6. **Cross-Border Transfers**
   - EU Commission 2021 SCCs (Module 2) attached
   - Transfer Impact Assessment commitments
   - Prohibition on storage outside approved regions

7. **Audit Rights**
   - Annual audit rights or SOC 2 report sharing
   - Breach-triggered audit rights
   - Reasonable notice and cost allocation

8. **Data Retention & Deletion**
   - Maximum retention period
   - Deletion verification mechanism
   - Backup retention exceptions

9. **Liability & Indemnification**
   - Liability for sub-processor actions
   - Indemnification for GDPR violations
   - Insurance requirements

10. **Termination**
    - Certified data deletion within 30 days
    - Return of data option
    - Survival of confidentiality obligations

**Annexes Required:**
- **Annex I:** Processing details (subject matter, duration, data types, data subjects)
- **Annex II:** Technical and organizational measures (security controls)
- **Annex III:** Sub-processor list
- **Annex IV:** EU Commission 2021 SCCs (Module 2)

## DPA Negotiation Process

### Step 1: Initial Contact (Day 1)
**Template Email:**
```
Subject: Data Processing Agreement Request - [Company Name]

Dear [Vendor Legal Team],

We are a current customer of [Vendor Service] and require a Data Processing
Agreement (DPA) to comply with GDPR Article 28.

We process personal data from EU/EEA data subjects and need to ensure our
vendor relationships meet regulatory requirements.

Please provide:
1. Your standard DPA for enterprise customers
2. EU Commission 2021 Standard Contractual Clauses (Module 2: Controller-to-Processor)
3. SOC 2 Type II report (if available)
4. List of sub-processors involved in data processing

Alternatively, please review our attached DPA template and advise on your
availability to execute.

Our account details:
- Account ID: [ID]
- Primary contact: [Name, Email]
- Legal contact: [Attorney Name, Email]

Target execution date: [Date - ideally 14 days]

Thank you,
[Legal/Compliance Team]
```

### Step 2: Document Review (Day 2-5)
- [ ] Receive vendor DPA or send our template
- [ ] Legal counsel review (use checklist above)
- [ ] Identify negotiation points
- [ ] Draft redlines and comments

### Step 3: Negotiation (Day 6-14)
- [ ] Send redlined DPA to vendor
- [ ] Schedule negotiation call if needed
- [ ] Escalate blockers to management
- [ ] Document all agreed changes

### Step 4: Execution (Day 15-21)
- [ ] Final DPA approved by both parties
- [ ] SCCs attached and completed
- [ ] Execute via DocuSign/Adobe Sign

### Step 5: Administration (Day 22+)
- [ ] File in contract management system
- [ ] Update vendor risk register
- [ ] Set calendar reminder for renewal (typically 3 years)

## SCC Completion Requirements

**EU Commission 2021 SCCs - Module 2: Controller-to-Processor**

### Mandatory Annexes

**Annex I: List of Parties**
- Data exporter details (our company)
- Data importer details (vendor)
- Contact information for both parties

**Annex I.A: Processing Details**
- Subject matter: [e.g., "Voice recording and transcription services"]
- Duration: [Term of DPA]
- Nature and purpose: [e.g., "Real-time speech-to-text conversion for customer support"]
- Type of personal data: [e.g., "Voice recordings (biometric), transcripts, metadata"]
- Categories of data subjects: [e.g., "End-user customers, employees"]
- Frequency of transfer: [e.g., "Continuous during service operation"]

**Annex I.B: Competent Supervisory Authority**
- [Country] Data Protection Authority
- [e.g., Irish DPC, German BfDI, etc.]

**Annex I.C: Controller Activities**
- Relevant GDPR activities: [e.g., "Voice agent service provider"]

**Annex II: Technical and Organizational Measures**

**Security Measures (Art. 32 GDPR):**
1. **Encryption:**
   - Data in transit: TLS 1.3 or higher
   - Data at rest: AES-256 or equivalent
   - Key management: HSM or equivalent

2. **Access Controls:**
   - Role-based access control (RBAC)
   - Multi-factor authentication (MFA)
   - Principle of least privilege
   - Access logging and monitoring

3. **Confidentiality:**
   - Employee confidentiality agreements
   - Background checks for personnel
   - Security awareness training

4. **Integrity:**
   - Data validation and checksums
   - Change management procedures
   - Version control

5. **Availability:**
   - Redundancy and failover
   - Backup procedures (frequency, retention)
   - Disaster recovery plan (RTO, RPO)

6. **Resilience:**
   - Regular security testing (pen tests, vulnerability scans)
   - Incident response plan
   - Business continuity plan

7. **Monitoring:**
   - Security Information and Event Management (SIEM)
   - Intrusion detection/prevention (IDS/IPS)
   - Log retention (minimum 12 months)

**Annex III: Sub-Processor List**
- Sub-processor name
- Location
- Processing activity
- Security measures

## Escalation Procedures

### Vendor Non-Responsiveness
**Timeframe:**
- Day 0: Initial contact
- Day 7: Follow-up if no response
- Day 14: Escalate to account manager/sales
- Day 21: Escalate to executive sponsor
- Day 30: Legal letter of intent to terminate
- Day 45: Service termination planning

### Unacceptable DPA Terms
**Process:**
1. Document unacceptable clauses
2. Propose alternative language
3. Schedule negotiation call
4. Escalate to senior legal if stalled
5. Consider vendor replacement if deal-breaker

### DPA Execution Delays
**Impact Assessment:**
- Risk of continued processing without DPA
- Regulatory exposure
- Business continuity impact
- Alternative vendor availability

**Decision Matrix:**
- **Low Risk + Delay:** Continue with aggressive follow-up
- **High Risk + Delay:** Immediate escalation to C-level
- **Critical Service + Delay:** Legal counsel opinion on interim safeguards
