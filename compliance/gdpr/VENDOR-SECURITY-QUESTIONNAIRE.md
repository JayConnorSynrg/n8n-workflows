## Appendix: Questionnaire Scoring Guide (Internal)

### Critical Questions (Deal-Breakers)

**Must-Have ("Yes" Required):**
- Q19: SOC 2 Type II certification (or equivalent ISO 27001 for non-US vendors)
- Q24: GDPR compliance confirmed
- Q33: Encryption at rest
- Q34: Encryption in transit
- Q53: Breach notification within 72 hours (preferably 24 hours)
- Q66: Confidentiality agreements for personnel
- Q80: Willing to execute DPA
- Q81: Willing to execute SCCs

**Unacceptable Answers (Red Flags):**
- Q18: "Yes, data used for training without opt-out" → Require contractual prohibition
- Q26: "Yes, enforcement action" → Require detailed investigation
- Q55: "Yes, breach" without adequate remediation → Require evidence of fixes
- Q79: "No, will not challenge government requests" → May require supplementary measures

### Risk Scoring Matrix

**Low Risk (0-20 points):**
- All critical questions answered satisfactorily
- SOC 2/ISO certified
- EU data residency available
- No breaches in 3 years
- Proactive security posture

**Medium Risk (21-50 points):**
- Most critical questions answered satisfactorily
- Security certifications present but some gaps
- US-based with SCC willingness
- Minor breaches with documented remediation

**High Risk (51-75 points):**
- Some critical questions with concerning answers
- Limited certifications
- Resistance to audit rights or SCCs
- Multiple breaches or poor incident response

**Critical Risk (76-100 points):**
- Multiple critical questions with unacceptable answers
- No security certifications
- Refusal to execute DPA/SCCs
- Active enforcement actions or major breaches

**Recommendation:**
- **Low Risk:** Fast-track DPA execution
- **Medium Risk:** Negotiate enhanced security terms in DPA
- **High Risk:** Require supplementary measures before approval
- **Critical Risk:** Do not approve vendor; seek alternatives
